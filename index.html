How it works

Three quick steps to get reminders that actually help.

Pick a place

Choose from curated places (e.g., Ghibli Park, Pokémon Café). More destinations added over time.

We compute timing

Based on opening schedules (e.g., “opens ~2 months before”), we calculate your reminder date.

Get notified

Receive an email reminder and export to your calendar (.ics). One click to the official booking page.
